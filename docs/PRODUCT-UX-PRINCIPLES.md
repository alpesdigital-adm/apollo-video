# Product and UX principles

The director proposes narrative, treatment and asset candidates. Rights, consent, budgets, schemas, timing and policy remain deterministic. Asset selection follows reuse → adapt → generate → omit, and `no_insert`, `no_effect` and `no_music` are valid decisions. Generated artifacts require criticism before promotion.

Manual edits, AI edits, undo and restore share the Command/Patch/Version model. Guardrails are structured and versioned. Capabilities are exposed externally only after their phase gate and never bypass policy.

Roles are operator/editor, director/strategist, administrator and reviewer. The server-side permission matrix is executable in `product-governance.ts`; the UI may hide unavailable actions but never acts as the authorization boundary.

The approved visual language uses a dark editorial canvas, restrained amber and sky accents, 44px targets, visible focus, keyboard support and reduced motion. Queued, running, review, failed, stale, completed and archived states have stable semantic presentation. Empty, loading, recoverable error and conflict states always explain the next safe action.
