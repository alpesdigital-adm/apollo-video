# Selective Apollo v1 reuse

Remotion scenes and subtitle components are adaptation candidates only after golden characterization and conversion to RenderInput/SubtitleStylePreset contracts. FFmpeg is extracted behind versioned recipes, typed timeouts/cancellation, staging and durable jobs. Whisper timing becomes a versioned adapter. Watchdog/progress becomes the generic workflow state machine.

The monolithic Claude service, universal Scene model, monolithic analyze/editor routes, JSON sources of truth and direct provider coupling are replaced. Image, video and stock services may enter only through Provider Registry contract suites. Every accepted extraction records origin, changes and owner, has before/after tests, removes direct v1 imports and deletes its temporary bridge only after v2 fixtures pass.
