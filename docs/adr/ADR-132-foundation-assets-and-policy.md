# ADR-132 — Foundation assets and policy kernel

Workspace identity is resolved server-side and all settings, brand kits, guardrails and policy snapshots are versioned. Legal/platform policy precedes workspace, project, briefing and learned preferences; same-level conflict blocks. Ingested content never enters the owner-instruction channel.

Media masters are immutable after streamed checksum verification. Every proxy, normalization or transformation is a content-addressed derivative with recipe/tool lineage. Source time metadata survives normalization. A central fail-closed rights evaluator protects every use boundary, while kind-specific contracts cover video, audio, image, document, synthetic and derivative assets.
